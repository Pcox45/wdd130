Rules of the Game

No running, jumping, or climbing on stage props or terrain in our designed rooms.

physical harm and verbal abuse will not be accepted

The game will be stopped and you will be asked to leave the property

Game Modes

Room 1: We have a standard deathmatch game mode. Maximum players are 20. We have two teams that will battle for the highest score. Each person has 3 lives, each elimination gives the entire team points. 3 Bonus points are given if you shoot the lights on the opponent's gun. The team with the most eliminations will win the game. Another way the team can secure victory is by eliminating the entire enemy team before the game ends in 15 minutes.

Room 2: We have captured the flag. Maximum players are 10. A player must carry the flag to their base. The flag is a virtual color code that can be captured when the player finds the opponent's console. The player must place his gun on the console which will change his gun to a different color. At that moment, the player must hurry back to his ally console and place his gun on the console to score 10 points. If he is eliminated the color code returns back to the console. The first team to 50 points wins the match. Players must guard the console and guard their teammates to win.

Room 3: Free for all. Maximum players are 15. Each person has 3 lives. A person is eliminated if the light tags on their body armor are shot. A player can receive 3 bonus points if they are able to shoot the light tags on an opponent's gun to eliminate them. Last man standing before 10 minutes of the game ending wins or the person with the most points at the end wins.
