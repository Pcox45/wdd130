Welcome Laser tag players

For ages 8 and older

Our mission is to provide you with a great place to battle your friends, family, and rivals in fun games of Laser-tag. We host 3 different rooms of Laser Tag; each room has its own game mode. Our second page has the rules of the games and more details on 3 different game modes you can play. Our 3rd page has information about rates and our business hours. Book your next battle on this page or our 3rd page.

What is laser tag? Lzer tag is a recreational shooting sport where players shoot infrared-emiting light guns to tag designed targets that change leds when shot at. Lazer tag games are held in dark rooms. Our rooms have backlight lights and designs for our stage effects.
